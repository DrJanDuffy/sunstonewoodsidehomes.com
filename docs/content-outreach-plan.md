# Content & Outreach Playbook

## Monthly content cadence
- **Week 1:** Publish a market pulse blog (Sunstone, Cadence, Summerlin inventory + incentives).
- **Week 2:** Spotlight a move-in-ready home with lifestyle photos; cross-link to detail page and contact form.
- **Week 3:** Share a neighborhood guide update (schools, commute, parks) with embeds from `/service-areas` pages.
- **Week 4:** Release a testimonial or concierge success story highlighting Dr. Jan Duffy’s process.

## Backlink & partnership targets
- Las Vegas relocation blogs (pitch Sunstone trail + master plan features).
- Local lifestyle publications (Weekend itinerary featuring Cadence + Lake Las Vegas).
- Mortgage and title partners (co-author financing explainer; request footer backlink on partner pages).
- Community associations (Sunstone, Cadence) for event recaps linking back to service area content.

## Social & email amplification
- Repurpose each long-form article into Instagram carousels + LinkedIn posts with CTA to `/move-in-ready`.
- Send a monthly Mailchimp campaign summarizing new inventory, incentives, and concierge wins.
- Encourage homeowner testimonials on Google Business Profile; embed quotes on homepage.

## Tracking & follow-up
- Log outreach emails and publication dates in CRM or Notion board.
- Monitor referral traffic in GA4 → Acquisition → Referral, tagging links with UTM parameters.
- Revisit high-performing posts quarterly and refresh stats/CTAs to maintain relevance.
